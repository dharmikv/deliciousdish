who we are

Our passion lies in curating immersive food tours that showcase the diverse culinary landscape of our city.

A culinary journey inspired by our passion for food and travel

Our love for discovering new cuisines and connecting with local communities has led us to create Delicious Dish Tours. With each tour, we aim to share the authentic tastes and cultural richness we’ve experienced during our adventures.

As owners and operators, we bring a wealth of firsthand knowledge and a deep appreciation for culinary diversity to every aspect of our tours. Drawing on our global experiences, we’ve curated a selection of immersive food experiences that highlight the best of local cuisine while providing insights into the stories and traditions behind each dish.
